Choose Your Class:

Lycan

Shapeshifting creatures of the night cursed to forever remain in this beastly state. Lycans are purely instinctive, relentless beasts. Stalk, howl, rip and tear.

Main attributes: Strength, Agility, Constitution.

Vampire

Either silver-tongued devils who always get what they want or shadowy nocturnal figures who only crave for blood. Concocting their vile plans under the night's cloak, vampires are both cunning and brutal.

Main attributes: Intelligence, Charisma.

Hunter

Humans both immune to the lycan curse and not descending of the vampire bloodline. Left alone to survive in this dark world, hunters are resourceful, unpredictable and errant figures.

Main attributes: Dexterity.

Necromancer

Pulled into this realm by occult rituals of a long gone Lord of the dark. Necromancers are unliving, chaotic individuals able to interact and control the dead. And given this long going war, there are plenty of lost souls going around...

Main attributes: Intelligence.

Sage

Akin to rural environments and magic, Sages are insightful beings from another realm that can channel and weaponize the forces of nature around them. Both rogues and mages, Sages are formidable individuals.

Main attributes: Dexterity, Intelligence.
